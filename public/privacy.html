Privacy Policy

BigBricey · Last updated July 2026

This product is invite-only. We collect the minimum needed to run your forever health log.

What we collect

  • Account: Google email, name, and profile photo (from sign-in)
  • Health & wellness: food logs, workouts, steps, weight, goals, height, age/sex for targets, freeform notes you enter
  • Permanent memory: facts and preferences you explicitly ask BigBricey to remember, plus where the memory came from so you can review, edit, or delete it
  • Product feedback you choose to send
  • Optional marketing preference (if you check it during setup)

How we use it

  • Power your personal log and daily dashboard
  • Store your forever ledger so history isn’t lost
  • Estimate calorie/macro targets from the stats you provide
  • If you opt in, occasional product updates (you can opt out)

What we don’t do

  • We don’t sell your health data
  • Other members cannot see your food log or body metrics
  • Admin feedback inbox only shows suggestions you send — not your diary

Storage & security

Data is stored in a cloud database with access controlled by your signed-in session. Service providers (hosting, auth, AI inference) process data only to run the product.

Your choices

You can update onboarding answers in the app. To delete your account or export data, contact Brice (the operator of this invite-only beta).

Contact

Questions about privacy: the account owner who invited you, or the operator of bigbricey.com.
